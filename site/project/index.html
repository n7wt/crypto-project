WHAT'S FIDELION?

In 2024, the world lost hope. The indiscriminate use of nuclear weapons had drastically changed the world. The systems that supported society and civilization were no longer functioning, and the world was plunged into a state of lawlessness. The survivors struggled to survive in the aftermath of the war, and 94% of humanity suffered greatly. Some were lucky enough to find shelter in underground bunkers, while others had prepared in advance, but all were desperate to survive. War is often fueled by anger, but the pain it causes lasts forever.
